How do I learn to sign myself?

Creating the first variants takes 3-5 working days. If you want to make changes, it will also take 3-5 business days. After you choose the final option, we will produce a personal tutorial for you.

My handwriting is really bad. Can I still learn how to sign?

Your handwriting has no effect on your new signature. Our tutorials are simple and very efficient. You will also improve your motor skills while learning your new signature.

How long does the signature creation take?

You will be able to learn how to sign yourself in an hour of practice with the help of our video tutorial and personal training stencil. In the video, you will see a step-by-step explanation of how to repeat and do the signature like how our calligraphers do them.

Do I need to change my documents?

If you change your signature, you do not need to change the signature in your passport, bank cards, contracts and other documents. New signature will have the same legally binding power as your current one.

The fact that the document was signed by you and not by someone else is determined by your handwriting, not by your signature, because it can be easily forged.

What if I don't like the offered signatures?

If you don’t like the signatures provided, we will offer you more options. You will be able to say what you prefer to change, and our calligrapher will redo the work until you feel that it's perfect
